About Me

Web Designer & Developer based in London

My name is Christopher Harvey, I am from Manchester, UK, and currently living in London where I am attending SAE Institute, studying a BSc (Hons) Web Development. My journey in Web Development began through self-teaching where I used programmes such as Udemy, FreeCodeCamp and Codeacademy in order to assist my learning. Through this, I built my portfolio and was then accepted at SAE Institute. As a student, I now focus my time and effort into building a career in Full Stack Web Development.
